My Education

Zangthi Primary School (2009–2015)

My educational journey began at Zangthi Primary School in 2009, where I studied from class PP to class 6, completing my primary education in 2015. The school, located in Lauri Gewog, Samdrup Jongkhar Dzongkhag, laid the foundation for my learning journey.

Minjiwoong Central School (2016–2022)

For my secondary education, I attended Minjiwoong Central School, where I studied from class 7 to class 12 and successfully graduated in 2022. This school played an important role in shaping my academic skills and preparing me for higher studies.

Samtse College of Education (2023–Present)

I am currently pursuing a B.Ed in Information Technology (Secondary) at Samtse College of Education. This program is helping me build both teaching and technical ICT skills, including programming, databases, and digital tools, which I aim to apply in my future career as an educator.
